IRS Proposes Treating HCSM Shares as HRA-Reimbursable

A proposed IRS rule would let HRAs reimburse employees tax-free for health care sharing ministry payments, direct primary care fees, and certain public coverage — classifying HCSM shares as an eligible medical expense without redefining them as insurance. A meaningful tailwind for the roughly 1.7 million Americans currently enrolled in sharing ministries.
