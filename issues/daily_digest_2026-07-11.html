IRS Guidance
IRS Proposes Allowing HRAs to Reimburse Health Sharing Fees Tax-Free
The IRS issued a proposed rule that would allow employer Health Reimbursement Arrangements (HRAs) to reimburse HCSM "shares" tax-free, alongside DPC fees and certain public coverage payments. This is a significant legitimizing step: employers could offer health sharing coverage as a benefit with the same tax treatment as traditional insurance. The rule also classifies DPC fees and HCSM shares as qualified medical expenses — potentially opening HSA and FSA pathways for these costs. For the ~1.3 million Americans currently in HCSMs, this represents a meaningful expansion of benefit access through employer channels.
