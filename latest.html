Direct Primary Care Market Grows to $75B

The DPC market is projected to grow from $70.17B in 2025 to $75.11B in 2026 (7.0% CAGR), driven by insurance administrative costs, physician burnout, and shrinking primary care appointment availability. IRS Guidance
Proposed Rule Would Let HRAs Reimburse Health Sharing Costs Tax-Free

A new IRS proposed rule would classify health care sharing ministry "shares," DPC membership fees, and some public coverage payments as tax-deductible qualified medical expenses reimbursable through HRAs. It doesn't reclassify sharing ministries as insurance, but it's a meaningful tax-treatment upgrade for the roughly 2 million Americans using HCSMs.

Take Command Health, 2026

Congress
Health Care Sharing Ministry Tax Parity Act Reintroduced

Reps. Kelly, Murphy, and Smith have reintroduced the Health Care Sharing Ministry Tax Parity Act, which would amend the Internal Revenue Code (companion to H.R. 2062) to formally treat HCSM membership — including administrative fees — as a deductible medical expense. Combined with the IRS's proposed HRA rule above, this is the closest health sharing has come to full tax parity with traditional insurance premiums.
